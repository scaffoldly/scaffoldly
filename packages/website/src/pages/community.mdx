Scaffoldly is an **open-source framework**. Our goal is to build a vibrant community as large as Terraform, Serverless, or Kubernetes.

We are licensed under the **[Functional Source License, Version 1.1, Apache 2.0 Future License](https://github.com/scaffoldly/scaffoldly?tab=License-1-ov-file)** which allows you to use Scaffoldly for free, even in commercial projects.

## How to Participate

### Star Us on GitHub

We'd love it if you could **star Scaffoldly on GitHub**. It helps us grow and reach more developers.

![GitHub Repo stars](https://img.shields.io/github/stars/scaffoldly/scaffoldly?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&logoColor=%23ffffff&link=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fscaffoldly%2Fscaffoldly)

### Discussions

We use [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/scaffoldly/scaffoldly/discussions) for feature requests, bug reports, and general questions.

![GitHub Discussions](https://img.shields.io/github/discussions/scaffoldly/scaffoldly?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&logoColor=%23ffffff&cacheSeconds=30&link=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fscaffoldly%2Fscaffoldly%2Fdiscussions)

### Discord

Join our [Discord Server](https://discord.gg/7FTY8CPPfZ) to chat with the team and other users.
